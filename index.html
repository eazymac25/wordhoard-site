Wordhoard

A quiet, offline vocabulary companion.

One considered word a day — with pronunciation, part of speech, definition, and an example sentence. Keep it in view with Home and Lock Screen widgets, and make it stick with flashcards and custom study lists.

No accounts · No ads · No internet required

Your pace, your level

Choose 1–4 words a day and a difficulty from Beginner to Expert, so the words you meet actually match where you are.

Track what you know

Favorite the words you love and mark words “known” to retire them from your rotation — the app only shows you words you haven't mastered.

Build study lists

Group words into custom lists and review them with a focused, distraction-free flashcard mode. Perfect for exam prep.

Private by design

No account, no sign-up, no analytics. Wordhoard works entirely offline and never asks for your data. Read the privacy policy.

Support

Questions, bug reports, or suggestions are always welcome. Email wordhoard.help@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

  • How do I change how many words I get each day? Open Settings (the gear icon on the Today screen) and choose 1–4 words per day.
  • How do I add the widget? Long-press your Home Screen, tap the +, and search for Wordhoard. Lock Screen widgets are added from the Lock Screen customizer.
  • Where is my data stored? Entirely on your device — see the privacy policy.
