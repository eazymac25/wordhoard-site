Wordhoard

A quiet, offline vocabulary companion.

Wordhoard brings you a considered word every day — with pronunciation, part of speech, definition, and an example sentence — plus Home and Lock Screen widgets, a browsable dictionary, favorites, and custom study lists with flashcard review. No accounts, no ads, no internet required.

Support

Questions, bug reports, or suggestions are always welcome. Email wordhoard.help@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

Frequently asked

  • How do I change how many words I get each day? Open Settings (the gear icon on the Today screen) and choose 1–4 words per day.
  • Can I pick a difficulty level? Yes — Settings lets you choose Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, or Expert, or leave it on All.
  • How do I add the widget? Long-press your Home Screen, tap the +, and search for Wordhoard. Lock Screen widgets are added from the Lock Screen customizer.
  • Where is my data stored? Entirely on your device. See our privacy policy.
